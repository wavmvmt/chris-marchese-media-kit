Chris Marchese is a Toronto and Miami-based luxury lifestyle and hospitality creator with a dedicated audience of over 360,000 followers across Instagram and TikTok. His content bridges the gap between aspirational travel and authentic storytelling.

Specializing in high-end resort experiences, private dining, and bespoke travel adventures, Chris has become the go-to voice for affluent millennials seeking curated luxury experiences in North America and beyond.

With a background in marketing and a refined aesthetic eye, every piece of content is crafted to convert — turning viewers into guests, guests into advocates.
